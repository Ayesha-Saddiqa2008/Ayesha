The Haas Underwater Basket Weaving Club

The Underwater Basket Weaving Club organizes activities to assest members in evaluating careers in basket weaving and identifying current carrer opportunities, extend the practical underwater education with job specific knowledge and connect members with alumni and other weaving professionals.

Popular Events

Some of the club's more popular events include the Underwater Ball in the fail, a recuriting tript to the Caribinan, Friday visits to local basket firms, happy hour gatherings with alumni, information sessions about careers/companies, interview preperation sessions and educational sessions on job functions.

Join Us

Weather you are interested in technology baskets weaving in Silicon Valley, running the corporate wear chest at the Great Barrier Roof, or leveraged bycotts on alumni beaches, the Underwater Basket Weaving Club organizes activities to ensures that members are prepared to achieve the goals within the full spectrum of available opportunities at Haas.
